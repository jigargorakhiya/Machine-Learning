Logistic Regression:
- It's one of the techinque to solve the classification problems
- Ex: 1. Binary Classification: Customer will buy insurance or not
      2. Multuple Classification: Person will give a vote to BJP, Congress, AAP or Other Parties

- Sigmoid or Logit Function:

  sigmoid(z) = 1/ ( 1 + e ^ -z )

            Where e = Euler's number ~ 2.71828

- Sigmoid function convert input into range 0 to 1.
